For Credit Card acceptance for donations and membership via website, Costs from several sources have been compared, the PTSA’s current banking provider, PayPal and two groups that serve the PTA community exclusively.  Given that we have sporadic usage it is in our best interest to use a service with zero monthly and/or minimum fees.  Both PayPal and Paperless PTO meet the criteria at similar cost.  Given most people’s familiarity with PayPal and the service’s ability to process transactions using both PayPal and Visa/MasterCard, Vicki recommends PayPal as the appropriate solution.  If our transaction volume and frequency grows significantly we can consider a more traditional provider.
