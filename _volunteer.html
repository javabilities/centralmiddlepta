Volunteers needed!

How can you become involved in your child's education? Become a Volunteer! There are many opportunities to volunteer at Central Middle School. Check out the list below to see if anything interests you, then contact our Volunteer Coordinator Kate Jones kateanddonjones@yahoo.com and let her know that you would like to help. Everyone who wants to volunteer in the schools needs to watch the Mandatory Sexual Harassment and Abuse video. Follow the link below to watch the video, then print up the form at the end of the video and send a signed copy into the front office.

Here is the link to the Sexual Harassment and Child Abuse video - when you click on this link, scroll down to the ***NEW - New volunteers can view the Sexual Harassment and Child Abuse presentation" then you click on the Windows Media site for the video or the Spanish version.
