Cercle Francais (French Club)

Day: Thursdays

Time: 2:55 - 3:55 (September - May)

Location: Room 104

Description: This club is open to 8th grade French students by contract only. Officer leadership positions are available. The goal is to promote a greater understanding of and appreciation for the French language and culture both globally and within the Central Middle community. Activities include (but are not limited to) National French Week awareness, cultural holiday celebrations, crafts, food, movies, sports, dances, there are/restaurant field trip(s), and an educational materials exchange with Chateaucoin Middle School in Bauge', France. Live Strong! Live French!

Teacher Sponsor: Mrs. Yuan (eyuan@aacps.org)

Writing Club

Day: Tuesdays and Thursdays

Time: 2:55 - 3:55

Location: Room 223 and Stem Office

Description: The Central Middle School Writing Center (The CWC) supports student writers by creating an environment where students can grow and develop skills and confidence, establish an independent authorial voice, collaborate, and form strategies for success. Our individualized, peer facilitated sessions focus on the writing process, encouraging conversation, revision, and reflection. In this process, students will not only develop their writing skills but also analytic and creative thinking.
